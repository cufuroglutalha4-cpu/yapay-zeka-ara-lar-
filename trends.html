General-purpose models stay at the center

Usage trend

Most people start with a general AI assistant and then add specialised tools on top. For many everyday tasks, a single model is enough ‚Äì but for deep work, people often combine multiple tools.

Specialised tools grow around them

Ecosystem

Instead of one model doing everything perfectly, people rely on a small stack of tools, each good at something specific.
