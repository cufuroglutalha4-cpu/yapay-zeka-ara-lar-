10 practical prompts to get more value from your AI assistant

If you are not sure what to ask your AI assistant, start with reusable prompt patterns. Below are ten ideas you can copy, paste and adapt for your daily work.

1. Summarise a long text

“Summarise the text below in 5 bullet points. Focus on the main decisions and risks.”

2. Rewrite in a different tone

“Rewrite this email in a polite, concise tone suitable for a business context.”

3. Generate ideas with constraints

“Give me 10 ideas for blog posts about AI tools, aimed at beginners, each with a one-sentence description.”

4. Explain a concept to a specific audience

“Explain what an API is to someone who is not technical, using simple analogies.”

5. Debug or understand an error

“Here is an error message and the related code. Help me understand what is going wrong and suggest a fix.”

6. Compare options

“Compare the pros and cons of using a relational database vs a NoSQL database for this scenario: [describe your project].”

7. Create a step-by-step plan

“Create a step-by-step 7-day study plan to learn the basics of [topic], including daily tasks and time estimates.”

8. Check clarity and structure

“Review this paragraph and suggest improvements for clarity and structure. Keep the same meaning.”

9. Role-play a scenario

“Act as a customer who is unhappy with a delayed order. I will reply as support. Help me practice handling this situation.”

10. Turn notes into a more polished output

“Here are my rough notes from a meeting. Turn them into a clean, structured summary with sections for decisions, open questions and next actions.”
Tip: Add context whenever possible – who you are, what you are trying to achieve, and what format you want in the answer (bullets, table, steps, etc.).
